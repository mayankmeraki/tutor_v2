Wave Interference

When two waves meet, they combine. If they're in phase — peaks align — they amplify each other. That's constructive interference.

If they're out of phase — peak meets trough — they cancel. Destructive interference.

This is why noise-cancelling headphones work. They generate the exact opposite wave to cancel ambient sound.
