What is Quantum Mechanics?
The Big Idea:
"Nature is not deterministic at the smallest scales. We can only predict probabilities."

• Know position & velocity
• F = ma tells the future
• Deterministic
QUANTUM
• Know ψ (wave function)
• iℏ∂ψ/∂t = Ĥψ tells the future
• Probabilistic
Energy landscape → dictates how ψ evolves in time
