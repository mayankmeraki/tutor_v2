Four whiteboard styles, one paradigm.

All four share the same idea — a single shared canvas where Euler and you are co-authors, with a tool palette, a beat timeline, pinned references, and a floating ask bar. They differ in material, spatial model, and where the tools live. Same lesson — Faraday → transformers — rendered four ways.
