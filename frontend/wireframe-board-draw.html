Tutor's Board LIVE DRAWING
1.0×

How this would work with the AI

The tutor emits a <teaching-board-draw> tag containing a sequence of drawing commands. The frontend animates them naturally — stroke by stroke — like watching someone draw on a blackboard.

Command types: line arrow arc rect circle text latex freehand pause voice (tutor narration shown as overlay)

The tutor's words appear at the bottom as they "speak" while drawing — combining visual and verbal explanation simultaneously, just like a real lecture.

Example tag:
<teaching-board-draw title="Forces on a Block">
{"cmd":"voice","text":"Let me draw the forces acting on this block..."}
{"cmd":"rect","x":300,"y":200,"w":100,"h":70,"color":"white"}
{"cmd":"arrow","from":[350,270],"to":[350,370],"color":"yellow","label":"mg"}
...
</teaching-board-draw>
